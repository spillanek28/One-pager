Why are Oceans Important?

10/31/24
Our oceans

About 70% of Earth's surface is covered by ocean. That is over half of the world covered. You may be wondering why do we need to worry about them? What are we doing wrong? Only some of the many problems are ocean dumping, overfishing, pollution, oil drilling, rising temperatures and so many more things. So many species are going to die out unless we put a stop to this.

If you are looking to show support for our sealife look down below.

How can we help?

10/31/24
Help Reasources

We can help by funding organizations that work to clear oceans from pollution and help challenge climate change to protect our oceans and the life inside of them. There are many organizations that work towards these goals such as Ocean Conservancy, Oceana, Ocean Clean up, Coral Reef Alliance, Project AWARE and so many more. It is as simple as donating even a dollar. Anything helps us clear our oceans for our futures so start donating now. Below we have some websites listed that are open to donations.
